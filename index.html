Biography

I am a researcher at the Korea AI Safety Institute (AISI). I received my Ph.D. from KAIST in 2026, advised by Prof. Junmo Kim. My research focuses on AI Safety (Deepfake Detection) and Efficient Learning (Dataset Condensation). I aim to build robust AI systems that are both safe for deployment and efficient to train.
