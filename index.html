Biography

I am a researcher at the Korea AI Safety Institute (AISI), working within the Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI). I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from KAIST in 2026.

My research addresses critical challenges in Artificial Intelligence Safety. Specifically, I focus on:

  • Deepfake Detection: Explainable frameworks for identifying synthesized media.
  • Dataset Condensation: Creating efficient, representative datasets for scalable learning.
  • Generative Model Safety: Controlling and detecting biases in large-scale models.
