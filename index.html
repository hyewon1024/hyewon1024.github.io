About

I am a Researcher at the Korea AI Safety Institute (AISI) within ETRI. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from KAIST in 2026, where I was advised by Prof. Junmo Kim.


My research interests lie in distribution matching (dataset condensation and domain adaptation/generalization), AI Safety (Explainable Deepfake Detection), and generative models.


During my M.S., I was a member of the VC Lab at SKKU, supervised by Prof. Jae-Pil Heo. I am actively seeking to apply my research in practical settings and contribute to innovative AI safety standards.
