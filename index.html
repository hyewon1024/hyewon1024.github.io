Biography

I am a Ph.D. graduate from KAIST, where I was advised by Prof. Junmo Kim. My research centers on **AI Safety** and **Efficient Learning**, specifically focusing on explainable deepfake detection, dataset condensation, and domain generalization.

Currently, I am a researcher at the Korea AI Safety Institute (AISI), working on robust frameworks for detecting AI-generated misinformation. I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical AI safety and practical, scalable deployment.
