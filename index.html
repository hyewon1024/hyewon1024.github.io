About

I am a Ph.D. student at the SIIT Lab at KAIST, supervised by Prof. Junmo Kim, interested in distribution matching (dataset condensation and domain adaptation / generalization) and generative models. During my M.S., I was a member of the VC Lab at SKKU, supervised by Prof. Jae-Pil Heo. I am actively seeking internship opportunities to apply my research in practical settings and contribute to innovative AI projects.
